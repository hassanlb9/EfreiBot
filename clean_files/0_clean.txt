christopher breaux (born october 28, 1987), ocean began his musical career as a ghostwriter, prior to joining the hip hop collective odd future in 2010. in 2011, ocean released his critically successful debut mixtape nostalgia, ultra and subsequently secured a recording contract with def jam recordings.
drawing on electro-funk, ocean's debut album channel orange (2012) was one of the most acclaimed albums of 2012. it was nominated for album of the year and won best urban contemporary album at the 2013 grammy awards, while the album's hit single thinkin bout you garnered ocean a nomination for record of the year.
after a four-year hiatus, ocean released the visual project endless (2016) to fulfill contractual obligations with def jam, among his accolades, ocean has won two grammy awards and a brit award for international male solo artist.
he was named by time as one the world's most influential people in 2013, while his albums have been listed in rolling stone's 500 greatest albums of all time (2020).
outside music, ocean has collaborated as a photographer with vogue and i-d, and founded the luxury brand homer.
